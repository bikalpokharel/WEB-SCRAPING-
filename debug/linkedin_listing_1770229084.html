          Key Responsibilities

          • Audit & Compliance
          • Ensure 100% physical verification audits for raw materials, packaging, finished goods, and fixed assets across factories, third-party locations, and distribution centers.
          • Support statutory and group audits by facilitating documentation and timely closure.
          • Coordinate specialized audits (Plastic, EWF, PACE, CAD) and ensure compliance with recommendations.
          • Maintain adherence to R2R/MTD processes and approval protocols.
          • Financial Reporting & Analysis
          • Prepare and present monthly business performance updates (cost, savings, margins) to factory leadership and corporate teams.
          • Submit global and external reports including production cost, technical efficiency, and variance analysis.
          • Provide internal reports on production volume, factory costs, and category performance.
          • Payment & Approval Management
          • Manage daily approvals for SCB payments, LC, shipping guarantees, and import duties.
          • Ensure timely confirmation of LC forward rates and maturity extensions.
          • Business Partnering & Cost Management
          • Track and review factory, logistics, and business waste costs; highlight variances and concerns.
          • Support budgeting and forecasting processes, including NMSCC cost constructs and capex planning.
          • Collaborate with procurement and operations teams for agreement closures and cost negotiations.
          • Forecasting & Savings Initiatives
          • Coordinate monthly cost forecasts based on S&OP and financial volumes.
          • Drive savings initiatives through SRS/NPP portal updates and workshops to identify cost-reduction opportunities.

          Required Qualifications & Skills

          • Bachelors degree in Finance, Accounting, or related field (MBA preferred).
          • 5+ years of experience in supply chain finance or similar roles.
          • Strong knowledge of financial reporting, audits, and compliance processes.
          • Proficiency in MS Excel, SAP, and financial planning tools.
          • Excellent analytical, problem-solving, and stakeholder management skills.

          Preferred Qualifications

          • Experience in FMCG or manufacturing industry.
          • Familiarity with global reporting standards and cost optimization strategies.
